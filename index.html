Why Travel with Us,?

Experience the adventure the way you want You will find inspiration and joy, enjoying the breathtaking scenery on foot, on a sea kayak, or by bike, all at your own pace. Choose from our many options to build your own journey of a lifetime When you think of Malaysia’s capital city, Kuala Lumpur, what usually comes to mind is a busy city with endless air-conditioned shopping malls. But there is more to KL than just designer shops and eateries. Part of the city’s attractions are the many lesser -known places for sensation seekers to get their adrenaline pumping. If you’re tired of thinking about spending another weekend in a busy mall, we’ve compiled a list of adventure activities you can try. From flying boarding to cage diving with sharks, we are confident that one of these activities will drive your adrenaline.

Listed here are some of the popular Activity in Kuala Lumpur Malaysia :

  • Tandem Skydiving
    • Tandem Skydiving harnesses you to an experienced skydiver, helpful if you’re a beginner. It is the amongst the most adventurous things to do in Kuala Lumpur. Majority providers of this activity provide you with equipment and videos of your adrenaline pumping flight. Keep in mind that this activity is seasonal and depends majorly on weather conditions as well.

  • Bungee Jumping
    • One of the most adventurous activities that you can try out when you come to Malaysia is Bungee Jumping. Lots of tourists from all over the globe come here to try this.

  • Flyboarding
    • The Flyboarding Experience at Marina Putrajaya renders a great chance of viewing the scenic landscapes of the city and fly up in the air with grabbing the beautiful sights. Feel the breeze and fly like a bird with no risks of falling down. This is surely going to be a memorable experience, accompanied by great safety and care. You shall be assisted with a professional instructor who will guide you throughout the activity session with the basic.

  • Hot Air Balloon
    • This adventure is very much suitable for just about anyone, meaning young kids are able to go for this experience. In general, it is also a great tourism tool for any visitors to Malaysia, or even for those wanting to do a unique wedding proposal, honeymoon, birthday or anniversary present. Currently, you can only do this in Putrajaya, Malaysia.

  • Crab Climbing
    • Move beyond plastic and ply and put your skills to the test on the terrain that gave birth to this sport. Come explore the outdoors with us, climbing on real rock will push you to expand your repertoire of techniques.

  • Shark Diving
    • Experience an educational journey that boosts your knowledge with our exclusive guided tour. This tour is conducted in English andour team of experts will be obliged to answer any questions pertaining to the aquarium at the end of each session.

  • Scuba diving
    • The diversity of the marine ecosystem around these islands makes them superb for underwater explorations and also sports such as water-skiing, boating, cruising, swimming and also diving.

  • Hiking
    • Enjoy nature at Forest Research Institute Malaysia For a small fee, visitors can enjoy various activities such as jogging, hiking,cycling or a leisurely stroll through the beautiful gardens here. From Kuala Lumpur, it is easy and convenient to reach many of these diving destinations.
